    Usage

    Input whatever you want to calculate. A suffix x denotes that the number is exact and its significant figures will not be taken into account. Everything other than numbers, parentheses, operators, and x is ignored, so you can easily copy-paste from other sources.

    Caveats

    Order of operation is not implemented, so 1 + 2 * 3 returns 9 instead of 6.

    Examples

    • 25.000 degrees Celsius to degrees Fahenheit: 25.000 * 1.8x + 32x
